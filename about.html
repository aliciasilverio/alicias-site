My passion for coding began with my love for video games. The first game I ever played was the original "God of War," and it sparked my curiosity about the technology and mechanics behind these virtual experiences. I spent countless hours exploring and experimenting with different programming languages, and I found that I had a natural talent for coding.

As a result, I decided to pursue a career in web development, where I could combine my passion for creating and my love of coding. I have honed my skills in JavaScript, as well as other languages such as Python, and I am currently in the process of learning Typescript to further expand my abilities.

I have had the opportunity to visit many amazing places and have experienced a wide range of cultures and traditions.

Overall, my interests in coding, traveling, and music have shaped who I am as a person and as a professional. I am always seeking new challenges and opportunities to grow and learn, and I am excited to see where my interests will take me next.
